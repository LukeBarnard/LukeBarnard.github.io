About CV Publications



I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Univerity of Reading, and my research focuses on how the Sun creates and controls the space environment near Earth. Some of the research questions I am working on include:

  • What are the limits to the predictability of space weather, and what factors influence this?

  • How can we best use heliospheric remote sensing data to improve space weather forecasting?

  • How does the long-term variability of the Sun affect the near-Earth space environment?

  • How can we improve estimates of past space climate from proxy observations, such as sunspot drawings, geomagnetic activity records, and cosmogenic isotope records?
