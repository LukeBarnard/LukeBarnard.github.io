I am a postdoctoral researcher and my research focuses on how the Sun creates and controls the space environment near Earth. Some of the research questions I am working on include:

  • What are the limits to the predictability of space weather and what factors influence this?
  • How can we best use heliospheric remote sensing data to improve space weather forecasting?

I tackle these questions using a combination of solar wind numerical models, heliospheric remote sensing instruments, and novel data analysis methods enabled by citizen science projects.
